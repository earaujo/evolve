What Evolve offers?

Genetic algorithms are implemented and some breeding and mutation strategies are also available. The currect implementation doesn’t offer much customization, but we’re working on that.

What comes next?

Through an integration with a Webapp, create a full stack for evolutionary computation test tool. There are also new algorithms coming: particle swarm and ant colonies.

May I contribute?

Sure you can. We're trying to struggle C language to offer an elegant and full speed archicture to solve non-trivial computational problems through evolutionary computation.
